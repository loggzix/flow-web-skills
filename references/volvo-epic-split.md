# Volvo Trucks — The Epic Split (77s, 2013) — pattern H2.3

## Cấu trúc
- Hook (0-13s): extreme close-up mặt JCVD nhắm mắt, voiceover trầm tự sự — chưa thấy gì xung quanh.
- Setup (14-20s): dolly-out lộ hai chân đứng trên 2 gương chiếu hậu xe tải.
- Demo (21-32s): wide shot 2 xe tải chạy LÙI song song.
- Climax (33-57s): 2 xe tách dần, Enya "Only Time" nổi lên, JCVD split 180° — không thoại.
- Reveal (58-77s): chữ giải thích "Volvo Dynamic Steering" + disclaimer chuyên gia.
- Kỹ thuật lõi: MỘT cú dolly-out liên tục từ close-up → extreme wide (tiết lộ dần); tính năng chứng minh bằng stunt; nhạc êm đè lên hành động nguy hiểm; golden hour ngược sáng.

## Prompt Veo tiêu biểu

### Prompt 1 (hook close-up)
Time: 8 seconds. Camera: Extreme close-up of a man's face, eye-level, subtle slow dolly backward. Subject: Middle-aged action star, serious calm expression, eyes closed, blue denim shirt, black fingerless gloves. Action: Standing perfectly still, slowly opening his eyes at the end. Location: Empty asphalt runway at sunset. Lighting: Warm golden hour backlight, glowing rim light on shoulders and face. Visual Style: Cinematic film look, warm golden tones, soft contrast. Line: "I've had my ups and downs, my fair share of bumpy roads and heavy winds. That's what made me what I am today." Voice Tone: Low, deep, gravelly voiceover with soft mysterious synth pads.

### Prompt 4 (climax split)
Time: 8 seconds. Camera: Extreme wide shot, eye-level, continuous smooth dolly out. Subject: Two large gold trucks driving backward and a man between them. Action: The trucks slowly drift apart sideways while driving backward; the man smoothly performs a full horizontal split between their side-view mirrors. Location: Deserted runway under brilliant golden sunset. Lighting: Vibrant sunset backlight, sun directly behind the man, long shadows. Visual Style: Epic cinematic, warm gold and soft blue tones, clean composition. Line: None. Voice Tone: Dreamy ethereal female vocal music playing softly.

### Prompt 5 (reveal chữ)
Time: 8 seconds. Camera: Extreme wide, slow dolly out expanding the landscape. Subject: Two trucks far apart, man holding the split. Action: Trucks drive backward at constant speed. Location: Vast empty runway at sunset. Lighting: Muted golden hour, sun at horizon. Visual Style: Epic scale, high-end commercial grading. Line: Text on screen: "This test was set up to demonstrate the stability and precision of Volvo Dynamic Steering." Voice Tone: Soft melodic music fading out.

## Vì sao hiệu quả
- One-take không cắt = bằng chứng thật → uy tín tuyệt đối.
- Nhạc êm dịu đối lập stunt nguy hiểm → biến demo thành nghệ thuật.
- Tiết lộ dần (close-up → wide) giữ tò mò từng giây.
- Stunt CHÍNH LÀ tính năng (ổn định vô lăng) — demo không lời nói.
