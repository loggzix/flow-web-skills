# Snickers — Betty White (30s, Super Bowl 2010) — pattern H2.5

## Cấu trúc
- Hook (0-7s): cụ bà 88 tuổi chơi bóng bầu dục bùn lầy, bị quật úp mặt xuống vũng bùn — vô lý tức thì.
- Setup (7-17s): cãi vã trên sân lộ ẩn dụ: cầu thủ Mike chơi yếu "như Betty White" vì ĐÓI.
- Product reveal (17-20s): bạn gái đưa Snickers: "Eat a Snickers."
- Resolution match-cut (20-22s): CÙNG GÓC MÁY, bà già → Mike thật đang nhai: "Better?" / "Better."
- Double twist (22-27s): cụ ông khác (đeo suspender) bị đốn ngã — gag lặp lần 2.
- Packshot (27-30s): kẹo bẻ đôi kéo caramel slow-motion + "You're not you when you're hungry. Snickers satisfies."
- Kỹ thuật lõi: ẩn dụ tương phản cực hạn làm hook; MATCH-CUT giữ nguyên góc máy để thể hiện biến đổi trước/sau sản phẩm không cần giải thích.

## Prompt Veo tiêu biểu

### Prompt hook (tackle bùn)
Time: 8 seconds. Camera: Medium shot, low-angle, static. Subject: An elderly woman with short silver hair in a light-blue knit sweater, and a defensive football player in dark sportswear. Action: The player tackles the elderly woman hard, sending her flying face-first into a large mud puddle, thick brown mud splashing. Location: Muddy puddle on a public park field. Lighting: Natural overcast daylight. Visual Style: Detailed mud fluid physics, comedic slapstick realism. Line: None. Voice Tone: Loud muddy splash and heavy impact sound.

### Cặp prompt MATCH-CUT (quan trọng nhất)
Prompt TRƯỚC — Time: 8s. Camera: Close-up, side-angle. Subject: Muddy hands of the elderly woman holding a Snickers bar, her grumpy face in background. Action: Unwrapping the wrapper. Location: Park field. Lighting: Natural daylight. Visual Style: Product close-up, sharp packaging detail. Line: None. Voice Tone: Rustling plastic wrapper.
Prompt SAU — Time: 8s. Camera: Medium close-up, SAME angle as previous scene. Subject: A young man with mud on his face, gray t-shirt, holding a half-eaten Snickers bar, alert and satisfied. Action: Chewing, nodding in relief. Location: Same park field. Lighting: Same natural daylight. Visual Style: Match-cut transition, satisfying character change. Line: "Better." Voice Tone: Confident, refreshed young male voice.
→ Quy tắc: 2 prompt giữ NGUYÊN Camera/Location/Lighting, chỉ đổi Subject = người xem tự hiểu sản phẩm gây biến đổi.

### Prompt packshot
Time: 8 seconds. Camera: 3D close-up animation. Subject: A Snickers bar breaking in half showing caramel pulling apart, crunchy peanuts, soft nougat, white text on screen. Action: Smooth slow-motion break. Location: Solid brown brand-color background. Lighting: Bright studio lighting highlighting food textures. Visual Style: High-gloss commercial food porn packshot. Line: Voiceover: "You're not you when you're hungry. Snickers satisfies." Voice Tone: Deep warm authoritative male, short signature brand jingle.

## Vì sao hiệu quả
- Ẩn dụ tương phản cực hạn (bà già vs bóng bầu dục) = hook 3 giây.
- Match-cut = cơ chế sản phẩm không cần lời.
- Double twist giữ chân tới packshot.
- Slogan buộc chặt vấn đề (đói) với giải pháp (Snickers).
