# Apple — 1984 (60s, Super Bowl 1984, đạo diễn Ridley Scott) — pattern H2.4

## Cấu trúc
- Hook (0-16s): thế giới dystopia — dòng người trọc đầu đồng phục xám đi đều trong hầm, ánh xanh lạnh, giọng Big Brother qua loa.
- Setup (16-32s): cô gái áo trắng quần đùi ĐỎ RỰC vác búa tạ chạy trốn lính giáp đen — đốm màu duy nhất của cả video.
- Climax (32-48s): chạy vào hội trường, slow-motion xoay búa, ném vỡ màn hình Big Brother — nổ sáng trắng overexposed, gió thổi đám đông há hốc.
- CTA (48-60s): chữ đen chạy trên nền trắng tĩnh lặng + giọng đọc trầm + logo Apple cầu vồng.
- Kỹ thuật lõi: contrast màu là ngôn ngữ chính (đỏ vs xám xanh); sản phẩm KHÔNG xuất hiện — reveal gián tiếp; nhịp chậm → rượt đuổi → slow-motion climax → tĩnh lặng.

## Prompt Veo tiêu biểu

### Prompt 1 (hook dystopia)
Time: 8 seconds. Camera: Wide shot, slow dolly-in, low-angle, transitioning to panning across faces. Subject: A long line of bald men with expressionless faces, identical drab gray industrial jumpsuits, heavy boots. Action: Walking in slow rhythmic lockstep down a metallic futuristic tunnel, heads bowed. Location: Dystopian industrial metal tunnel with small monitors on walls. Lighting: Moody low-key cold blue, harsh white backlighting casting long shadows. Visual Style: 1980s cinematic film look, high contrast, heavy film grain, desaturated blue-gray palette. Line: "Today we celebrate the first glorious anniversary of the Information Purification Directives..." Voice Tone: Deep echoing authoritative male voice through PA speakers, low mechanical hum.

### Prompt 5 (slow-motion xoay búa)
Time: 8 seconds. Camera: Close-up of chasing guards, cutting to low-angle wide dynamic shot. Subject: Riot guards in black armor; an athletic blonde woman in white tank top and red shorts holding a sledgehammer. Action: Guards close in; the woman spins around multiple times building momentum, preparing to throw the hammer at the giant screen. Location: Front stage of a grand auditorium below a giant screen. Lighting: Intense cold blue spotlight on her, bright white light from the screen behind. Visual Style: Dramatic slow-motion, motion blur, action movie style. Line: "Our enemies shall talk themselves to death and we shall bury them with their own confusion. We shall prevail!" Voice Tone: Crazed screaming propaganda voice, her scream of exertion, wailing sirens.

### Prompt 6 (nổ màn hình)
Time: 8 seconds. Camera: Tracking shot following the flying sledgehammer, cut to wide shot of auditorium. Subject: Sledgehammer, giant CRT screen, crowd of bald men. Action: Hammer crashes into the screen causing a violent explosion of white light; workers sit in stunned shock, wind blowing their clothes. Location: Auditorium stage and seating. Lighting: Blinding overexposed white flash, then soft smoky ambient. Visual Style: Shattering glass particles, extreme contrast, cinematic slow-motion, smoke. Line: None. Voice Tone: Loud glass shatter, deep boom, rushing wind fading out.

### Prompt 7 (CTA chữ tĩnh)
Time: 8 seconds. Camera: Static graphic. Subject: Black scrolling text on off-white background. Action: Text scrolls: "On January 24th, Apple Computer will introduce Macintosh. And you'll see why 1984 won't be like '1984'." Lighting: Even flat. Visual Style: Clean minimalist retro typography. Voice Tone: Calm reassuring deep male voiceover.

## Vì sao hiệu quả
- Contrast màu cực độ: nhân vật đỏ-trắng vs thế giới xám → mắt tự tìm nhân vật chính.
- Pacing tăng dần: chậm ngột ngạt → rượt → nổ → tĩnh.
- Reveal gián tiếp: không thấy máy tính → tò mò tối đa.
- CTA tối giản sau cao trào → thông điệp khắc sâu.
