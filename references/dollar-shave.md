# Dollar Shave Club — Our Blades Are F***ing Great (93s, 2012) — pattern H2.2

## Cấu trúc
- Hook (0-17s): founder tự giới thiệu + giá sốc $1/tháng + câu chửi thề deadpan "Our blades are f**king great" (biển hiệu đỏ trên cửa).
- Demo (17-25s): thông số sản phẩm + visual gag nền (em bé cạo đầu người đọc "The Lean Startup").
- Chê đối thủ (25-44s): vợt tennis ($19 trả cho Federer), ảnh ông nội + xe nâng lùi hé lộ.
- Uy tín + quy trình (45-67s): bàn đóng gói, chém băng keo bằng mã tấu, nhân viên Alejandro, xe kéo đỏ.
- Climax CTA (67-81s): đi trên pallet góc thấp → thổi tiền + confetti trước cờ Mỹ, gấu mascot nhảy.
- Packshot (81-93s): đồ họa đen trắng "ISN'T IT ABOUT TIME?" + logo + slogan "SHAVE TIME. SHAVE MONEY."
- Kỹ thuật lõi: dolly-back đi lùi trước mặt founder XUYÊN kho hàng suốt video (one-take illusion); mỗi câu thoại = 1 luận điểm; gag chạy nền, founder không nhìn.

## Prompt Veo tiêu biểu

### Prompt 1 (hook văn phòng)
Time: 8s. Camera: Medium close-up, static, eye-level. Subject: Caucasian man late 20s, short brown hair, light blue shirt, loose grey tie, deadpan look. Action: Speaks directly to camera. Location: Cluttered office with wooden shelf of products. Lighting: Diffused soft front light. Visual Style: Documentary style, realistic warm tones. Line: "Hi, I'm Mike, founder of DollarShaveClub.com. What is DollarShaveClub.com? Well, for a dollar a month we send high quality razors right to your door." Voice Tone: Calm, confident, deadpan, no music.

### Prompt 3 (walkthrough + gag nền)
Time: 8s. Camera: Medium-wide, dolly back tracking. Subject: Man wearing a long orange cape, holding a classic razor; behind him a toddler sits on a man's shoulders shaving his head while he reads "The Lean Startup". Action: Man walks backward explaining product features; toddler spreads shaving cream. Location: Warehouse aisle with cardboard boxes. Lighting: Cool fluorescent overhead. Visual Style: High contrast, orange cape pops against grey-brown warehouse. Line: "Each razor has stainless steel blades, an aloe vera lubricating strip, and a pivot head. It's so gentle a toddler could use it." Voice Tone: Instructive, deadpan.

### Prompt 9 (climax ăn mừng)
Time: 8s. Camera: Wide shot, static. Subject: Man center holding a red leaf blower, giant American flag behind, dancing mascot bear left, employee right. Action: Blows fake dollar bills and confetti into the air; everyone dances. Location: Festive stage inside warehouse. Lighting: Bright warm celebratory studio lights. Visual Style: Vibrant, chaotic celebration. Line: "...and the party is on!" Voice Tone: Ecstatic shouting, loud EDM.

### Prompt 10 (packshot)
Time: 8s. Camera: Static graphics. Subject: Large white bold text and logo on black. Action: "ISN'T IT ABOUT TIME?" transitions to crossed-axes logo, website, slogan "SHAVE TIME. SHAVE MONEY." Visual Style: Minimalist high-contrast brand outro. Voice Tone: Silent, music fades out.

## Vì sao hiệu quả
- Hook giá + chửi thề deadpan phá phòng thủ người xem trong 15s.
- Dolly-back xuyên kho liên tục → tò mò cảnh kế, không tắt video.
- Tương phản cực đoan với đối thủ (đèn pin/cán rung vs 1 lưỡi dao tốt).
- Hài hình ảnh chạy nền (mã tấu, gấu, em bé) trong khi thoại vẫn bán hàng nghiêm túc.
- Climax bùng nổ rồi hạ màn bằng CTA đồ họa tối giản.
