# Old Spice — The Man Your Man Could Smell Like (30s, 2010) — pattern H2.1

## Cấu trúc
- Hook (0-11s): MC phòng tắm nói thẳng ống kính với khán giả NỮ (người mua sữa tắm cho chồng) + giơ sản phẩm.
- Setup (11-18s): phòng tắm trượt đi lộ du thuyền — chuyển không gian không cắt máy.
- Twist (18-25s): cận cảnh tay — vỏ sò → vé → kim cương chảy qua ngón tay.
- Payoff (25-32s): wide shot cưỡi ngựa golden hour + slogan + jingle huýt sáo.
- Kỹ thuật lõi: giả ONE-SHOT bằng dolly-back/pan liên tục; escalation phi lý tăng dần; MC đứng yên tương đối trong khung, thế giới biến đổi quanh anh ta.

## Prompt Veo mẫu (4 cảnh × 8s)

### Prompt 1
Time: 8 seconds. Camera: Medium shot, eye-level, static with subtle dolly. Subject: A fit, muscular Black man with a short beard, wearing only a grey towel, charismatic confident expression, direct eye contact with camera. Action: Stands in a shower speaking directly to the viewer; at the end lifts a red body wash bottle covered in suds. Location: Clean bathroom, beige tiles, running shower head, sink and mirror on the left. Lighting: Soft even neutral front light. Visual Style: Clean modern commercial, warm tone, photorealistic. Line: "Hello, ladies. Look at your man, now back to me, now back at your man, now back to me. Sadly, he isn't me, but if he stopped using lady scented body wash and switched to Old Spice, he could smell like he's me." Voice Tone: Deep, smooth, charismatic male.

### Prompt 2
Time: 8 seconds. Camera: Medium-wide, smooth crane, panning right and dollying back. Subject: Same man, white linen trousers, striped sweater draped over shoulders. Action: Walks forward on a boat deck as the background seamlessly transitions from bathroom to yacht, holding the red bottle. Location: Wooden deck of a luxury yacht on calm blue ocean. Lighting: Bright natural daylight. Visual Style: High-contrast vibrant premium commercial. Line: "Look down, back up, where are you? You're on a boat with the man your man could smell like." Voice Tone: Energetic, authoritative.

### Prompt 3
Time: 8 seconds. Camera: Close-up on hand and chest, static. Subject: The man's hand, deadpan humorous expression. Action: Opens an oyster revealing two tickets, tickets instantly transform into sparkling diamonds pouring through his fingers. Location: Yacht deck, ocean blurred behind. Lighting: Bright sunlight reflecting off diamonds. Visual Style: Sharp focus, high detail, commercial grade. Line: "What's in your hand, back at me. I have it, it's an oyster with two tickets to that thing you love. Look again, the tickets are now diamonds." Voice Tone: Fast-paced, surprising.

### Prompt 4
Time: 8 seconds. Camera: Wide shot, rapid dolly back. Subject: Same man sitting sideways on a white horse holding the red bottle, triumphant. Action: Sits perfectly still, posing heroically; horse stands on sand. Location: Tropical beach at sunset, palm fronds top right; red text on screen "SMELL LIKE A MAN, MAN. Old Spice". Lighting: Warm golden hour. Visual Style: Cinematic warm palette, iconic ad style. Line: "Anything is possible when your man smells like Old Spice and not a lady. I'm on a horse." Voice Tone: Deadpan triumphant, followed by whistle jingle.

## Vì sao hiệu quả
- Nhắm đúng NGƯỜI MUA (phụ nữ) chứ không phải người dùng.
- One-shot không điểm dừng → không có chỗ để người xem thoát.
- Escalation phi lý: phòng tắm → tàu → kim cương → ngựa.
- Sản phẩm luôn trên tay MC, jingle chốt nhận diện.
